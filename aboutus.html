Meet the Owner: Olanike Babalola

Olanike Babalola, the heart and soul behind Sokoyokoto, has always had a deep passion for Nigerian food. Growing up in a family where cooking was an integral part of everyday life, she learned the art of creating traditional, flavorful dishes from her grandmother. After years of honing her skills and perfecting recipes, Olanike launched Sokoyokoto with a vision to share Nigeria's rich culinary heritage with the world.

Our Story

At Sokoyokoto, Olanike aims to not just serve food, but to tell a story with every dish. From the spicy flavors of Ayamase to the comforting Gbegiri, every meal is a reflection of Olanike’s love for Nigerian food and culture. Her dedication to quality and authenticity ensures that each customer gets a taste of Nigeria in every bite.

With a commitment to supporting local farmers and using only the finest ingredients, Olanike has built Sokoyokoto into a place where everyone is welcome to experience the best of Nigerian cuisine, just as it would be prepared in her own home.

"Food brings people together, and I want everyone who visits Sokoyokoto to feel like they are part of the family." – Olanike Babalola
