Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver?
Yes, we deliver nationwide across Nigeria.
Are the meals fresh?
Absolutely. Our chefs prepare all meals fresh every day.
Do you have vegetarian options?
Yes, we offer several plant-based Nigerian dishes.
How do I place an order?
You can order directly through our website or via WhatsApp.
What payment options do you accept?
We accept card, transfer, and mobile payment options.
Is delivery free?
Delivery is free for orders over ₦5,000.
Can I customize my order?
Yes! You can leave special requests at checkout.
What is the average delivery time?
Delivery usually takes 30 to 60 minutes depending on location.
Do you cater for events?
Yes, we provide catering for weddings, parties, and corporate events.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team via the contact form or WhatsApp.
